# Limitations

This repo is an educational Python reconstruction of part of an investment competition strategy.

Important limitations:

- The event sample is small.
- Biotech catalyst returns are fat-tailed and noisy.
- Approval does not guarantee a positive return.
- CRLs do not always produce the worst return if the market already expected failure.
- Market prices already embed expectations, risk premia, launch uncertainty, financing risk, and label risk.
- Checklist scoring is a disciplined way to structure judgement, not a proven forecast model.
- The Cytokinetics case study is reconstructed from slide inputs and illustrative checklist scores.
- This is not investment advice and not a live trading strategy.
